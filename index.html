About me

My name is Maciej Senko and I am Polish. I have been born in small city: Nowogard. I have finished Uniwersity of Szczecin at 2010. My main subject was biology with laboratory diagnostics as speciality. I came to Scotland in 2012. I have met lots of friends and my wife. We have a beautiful child Tosia. At present time I am working as a shift manager in food factory which is mainly focused on slow cooking called Sous Vide. I am responsible for all logistics aspects and Sous vide departmemnt. Scotland has lots of beautifull views from lakes to amazing beaches, magical forests and great number of historical castles. In this website I will try to show you what places are good to see when you visit this beautifull country.
